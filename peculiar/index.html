as long as light
rolls bold through
our lives we leave behind
 and we strive so still til
the coming of the night

racing our minds graced with lines
left behind by the subtle phases
 and the places we've embraced
the stages we've ascended
headed for the grave or for
heavenly faces

distilled to poetry
